About W.

Celebrating excellence in web design and development since 2010.

W. was founded with a simple mission: to showcase and celebrate the very best in web design and development. What started as a small community of passionate designers has grown into a global platform that influences trends, connects professionals, and inspires the next generation of digital creators.

Our team of curators and judges includes industry veterans, award-winning designers, and technical experts who carefully evaluate each submission based on design, innovation, content, and technical execution. Being featured on W. has become a mark of excellence in the industry, and we're proud to have highlighted thousands of exceptional websites over the years.

Beyond our awards and nominations, we're committed to fostering a community where designers and developers can learn, share, and grow together. Through our Academy, Conferences, and Directory, we provide resources and connections that help professionals at every stage of their career.
